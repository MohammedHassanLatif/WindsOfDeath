Welcome!

Thank you for visiting the official website for Winds of Death! Here you can find everything you need to know such as who made it, how they made it and why!

The Game

Our game has been created in Phaser 3.55 "Ichika"! We've been using the Phaser Editor to make the process easier, Piskel to draw and animate graphics and Tiled to lay them out on the game world.

The Team

Our team consists of Aleksandar Marinov, Fraser McAulay, Mihai Maftei and Mohammed Latif. Each of us comes from a different background with our own diverse skill sets which we will put to use in making this game.

Designing the game

The design of our game has been influenced by many factors. Examples include popular design principles, our personal taste in games as well as other media such as films.
